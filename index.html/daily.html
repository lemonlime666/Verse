CASUAL SUITS

  • Higher armholes
  • Trim through the chest and shoulders
  • Fitted trousers
  • Tapered leg

Casual suits are intended to showcase a more relaxed, informal appeal. Rather than the typical, formal suits, these suits are designed in versatile styles and designs for casual wearing. They are also constructed using different types of materials and come in different sizes and colors. Casual suits are not as roomy as compared to regular fit suits.
